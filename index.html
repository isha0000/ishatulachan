Hello! We're BTS.

BTS stands for the Korean phrase Bangtan Sonyeondan (Korean: 방탄소년단; Hanja: 防彈少年團), which translates literally to "Bulletproof Boy Scouts."

The themes explored in BTS discography range from exploring "the troubles and anxieties of school-age youth" to "themes like love, friendship, loss, death, and more.
